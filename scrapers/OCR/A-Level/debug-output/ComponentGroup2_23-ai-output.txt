   3.3 the typical depiction of barbarians in classical Greek sources, including:

       3.3.2 their way of life and customs
       3.3.3 their character
       3.3.4 politics and hierarchy
       3.3.5 their religion and rituals
   3.4 the role of the historical context in this ‘invention’ of the barbarian, including:
