3.2. Assessment objectives (AO)
3.2.1. AO1: Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of the language
3.2.2. AO2: Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of literature and/or other ancient sources
3.2.3. AO3: Analyse, evaluate and respond to literature and/or other ancient sources

3.3. AO weightings
3.3.1. Language: 50% AO1
3.3.2. Prose Literature A: 12.5% AO2, 12.5% AO3
3.3.3. Prose Literature B: 12.5% AO2, 12.5% AO3
3.3.4. Verse Literature A: 12.5% AO2, 12.5% AO3
3.3.5. Verse Literature B: 12.5% AO2, 12.5% AO3
3.3.6. Literature and Culture: 12.5% AO2, 12.5% AO3

3.4.1. Total qualification time: 140 hours

3.5.1. Available in England
3.5.2. Language: English only
