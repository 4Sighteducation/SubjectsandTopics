1. Unit 1: Reading
   1.1 Content description
       1.1.1 Read for different purposes
       1.1.2 Understand a word, phrase, sentence or whole text in its context
       1.1.3 Read and understand a range of information and publicity texts
       1.1.4 Identify key information, draw comparisons and distinguish facts and ideas
       1.1.5 Read and understand a range of short opinion pieces
       1.1.6 Read and understand a range of factual texts
       1.1.7 Understand a wide range of grammatical forms and structures
       1.1.8 Understand a wide range of relevant and appropriate vocabulary
   1.2 Assessment information
       1.2.1 First assessment: June 2025
       1.2.2 The assessment is 1 hour
       1.2.3 Examination questions are set in English
       1.2.4 The assessment is out of 50 marks
       1.2.5 Each question is set in a context
       1.2.6 Learners must answer all questions
       1.2.7 The Reading unit assessment consists of six reading texts

   2.1 Content description

       2.1.3 Listen to and understand conversations
       2.1.4 Listen to and understand extracts from public presentations
       2.1.5 Listen to and understand a wide range of grammatical forms and structures
       2.1.6 Listen to and understand a wide range of relevant and appropriate vocabulary
   2.2 Assessment information
       2.2.1 First assessment: June 2025

       2.2.3 Examination questions are set in English
